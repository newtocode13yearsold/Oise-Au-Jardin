    About Oise au Jardin

    My name is Thomas Legros. I'm 14 years old and I built this platform to help young people like myself find small gardening jobs and earn pocket money. I made it completely free because when I was younger I always wanted to find gardening jobs but existing platforms were too expensive. This is also my first portfolio project!
