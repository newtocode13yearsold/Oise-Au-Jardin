    Our Story

    Built by a teen,
    for young people

    My name is Thomas Legros. I'm 14 years old and I built this platform because I wanted to find small gardening jobs myself — but every platform I found was expensive or too complicated. So I made my own, completely free.

    Oise au Jardin connects young gardeners looking to earn pocket money with local clients who need reliable help in their garden.

    🌱 This is also my first ever portfolio project!
    🌿

    Teens earning.

    How It Works

    Three simple steps

    Getting started takes less than 2 minutes

    🌱

    Find a Gardener

    Browse young gardeners in your area. See their services, hourly price, star ratings, and availability — then message them directly.

    🏡

    Post a Job

    Need garden help? Post your job in seconds. Describe the tasks, your budget and date — motivated gardeners will reach out.

    💸

    Always Free

    Oise au Jardin is completely free for everyone — no subscriptions, no commissions, no hidden fees. Ever.
