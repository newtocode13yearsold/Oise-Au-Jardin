    Everything you need to know about Oise au Jardin

    🌿 General

    Oise au Jardin is a free platform that connects young gardeners (teenagers looking for small jobs) with clients who need help in their garden. It was created by Thomas Legros, a 14-year-old from the Oise region of France. It's completely free for everyone — no fees, no subscriptions, ever.

    Yes, 100% free. Creating an account, posting jobs, posting your availability as a gardener, messaging other users — all of it is completely free. We don't take any commission or charge any fees.

    The platform is mainly focused on the Oise region in France (cities like Compiègne, Senlis, Creil, Beauvais, etc.), but anyone from anywhere can sign up and use it.

    🏡 For Clients

    Go to the Gardeners page and browse available young gardeners near you. You can filter by location, service type, price, and rating. Click "View Profile" on any card to see more details, then send them a message directly.

    Create a free account (choose "Client" when signing up), then go to the Jobs page and click "Post a Job". Fill in the location, what tasks you need done, your budget, and the date — and interested gardeners will be able to contact you.

    Oise au Jardin is only a platform to connect people — we don't handle any payments. You arrange payment directly with the gardener (cash, bank transfer, etc.) after agreeing on the job details in the chat.

    🌱 For Gardeners

    Click "Sign Up" at the top of the page and choose "Young Gardener" as your role. Then go to the Gardeners page and click "Post Your Availability" to create your gardener profile with your location, services, and hourly price.

    Any gardening work you're comfortable with — lawn mowing, weeding, hedge trimming, pruning, planting, leaf collection, watering, or general tidying. You can list whatever you offer when you create your availability post.

    That's entirely up to you. A typical rate for a young gardener in France is around €8–€15 per hour, depending on the task and your experience. You set your own price when you post your profile.

    🔒 Safety
